How to Make Semolina Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup semolina
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • A pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg (optional)
  • Fresh fruit, nuts, or honey for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the butter over low heat until melted.
  2. Add the semolina and stir continuously for 2-3 minutes until lightly toasted.
  3. Gradually pour in the milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Add the sugar and continue to cook on low heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract and cook for another minute.
  6. Pour the pudding into serving bowls and let it cool slightly.
  7. Garnish with cinnamon, fresh fruit, nuts, or a drizzle of honey, if desired.
  8. Serve warm or chilled as per your preference!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: ~250 per serving
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Tip: Use almond milk for a dairy-free version!
